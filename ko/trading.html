Trailing stops

Trailing stop activates once a position is up +2%. The stop follows the price upward and locks in profit if the market reverses. Configurable activation threshold per strategy.

📊

+3%로 축소

승리 위치에 도달하면 +3%, the engine adds to the position (scale-in) to maximise gains on trending moves. Maximum 3 scale-ins per position to control risk.

🔵 시장 관리

단단한 1% 정지 — in a sideways chop, wide stops give up too much ground before the market moves in your favour. Tighter exits protect capital and allow re-entry.

🟡 변동성 시장

더 넓어짐 3% 중지 — in high-volatility regimes, tight stops get hit by noise. The wider stop rides out normal swings while still protecting against a true reversal.
