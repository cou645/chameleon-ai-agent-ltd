SERVICE 2

🌍 Domain Name Configuration

DNS configuration is the part most people find confusing — registrar logins, record types, propagation waits. The AI handles it directly where possible and provides exact step-by-step guidance everywhere else.

  • Identify your registrar automatically or via a short questionnaire
  • Générez les enregistrements DNS exacts requis (A, AAAA, CNAME) dans un tableau copier-coller
  • Fournissez des instructions spécifiques au registraire : Squarespace, GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, 123-reg et plus
  • Plain-English explanation of every record type before you touch anything
  • Set custom domain via GitHub Pages API — no manual form entry
  • Surveiller la propagation DNS et confirmer l'émission du certificat HTTPS
  • Full automation for registrars that provide an API (Cloudflare, Namecheap)
⚠️
Pourquoi c'est important : In our own testing, domain configuration caused the most confusion — a domain had silently migrated from Google Domains to Squarespace with no obvious redirect, and the DNS record types (A, AAAA, CNAME) were completely unfamiliar. The AI now asks your registrar upfront and gives instructions that require no prior DNS knowledge.

SERVICE 5

📈 Visibilité et croissance

Getting found — search engines, directories and professional networks. The AI handles the technical SEO groundwork and helps you build a credible online presence without paid advertising.

  • Balises méta SEO, balisage Open Graph et Twitter Card sur chaque page
  • sitemap.xml et robots.txt générés et tenus à jour
  • Données structurées Schema.org (Organisation, Produit, ContactPoint)
  • Soumettre à Google Search Console et Bing Webmaster Tools
  • Analyses respectueuses de la vie privée – Plausible ou Umami auto-hébergées (aucune bannière de cookie n'est nécessaire)
  • AI-written blog posts and case studies to drive organic search traffic
  • LinkedIn / GitHub profile alignment with website content

Pourquoi c'est le service 5

A site that no one can find is worth little. Services 1–4 build and run the site. Service 5 makes it discoverable. SEO, structured data and analytics are all automatable by AI — no ongoing agency retainer needed.
