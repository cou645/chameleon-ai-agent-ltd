📌

Trailing stops

Trailing stop activates once a position is up +2%. The stop follows the price upward and locks in profit if the market reverses. Configurable activation threshold per strategy.

📊

缩减 +3%

当获胜位置达到 +3%, the engine adds to the position (scale-in) to maximise gains on trending moves. Maximum 3 scale-ins per position to control risk.

⏱️

时间退出

持仓量持平 (< ±0.3%) 30分钟 are closed automatically. Flat markets tie up margin and accumulate fees — time exits free capital for better opportunities.

🐳

鲸鱼意识策略

这 whale_aware_strategy.py 模块监控表明机构活动的大宗订单的订单簿深度。头寸的大小和时间安排是为了避免陷入鲸鱼走势的错误方向。

🔵 市场范围

紧的 1%停止 — in a sideways chop, wide stops give up too much ground before the market moves in your favour. Tighter exits protect capital and allow re-entry.

🟡 市场波动

更宽 3%停止 — in high-volatility regimes, tight stops get hit by noise. The wider stop rides out normal swings while still protecting against a true reversal.
