📞

Cầu gọi SIM

Already on a cellular call and want to bring in a P2P peer? Tap Thêm vào nhóm cuộc gọi — the app registers the WebRTC call with Android's telecom stack alongside the SIM call. Android presents a native "Merge calls" option; accepting it hands audio mixing to the telephony hardware. The merged call plays in the chỉ tai nghe — fully private, no speakerphone, no one in the room hears. When hardware conference isn't supported by the carrier, a Bluetooth headset bridge routes all audio through the BT SCO link instead.

Nó kết nối như thế nào

🔐

Tailscale-secured mesh

All channels run over your private Tailscale network. No ports are opened to the public internet. The phone must be authorised on your tailnet — everything else is end-to-end encrypted with WireGuard.

🔌

Single WebSocket bridge

Máy tính để bàn chạy chameleon_mobile_bridge.py as a daemon thread inside the Qt process. The phone app connects to port 8770 on the Tailscale IP. One persistent connection carries all channels as typed JSON messages.

🤖

Termux on Android

Máy chủ tín hiệu (chameleon_signal.py) can run directly on the Android phone via Termux — making the phone both a client and a signalling node. No cloud relay needed for WebRTC peer connections within your tailnet.

📶

Dự phòng mạng LAN

Khi Tailscale không thể bật được, lan_video.py truyền phát video qua UDP (cổng 47732) và âm thanh qua UDP (cổng 47733) trực tiếp trên mạng cục bộ — cùng các kênh, không có phí VPN.
