Manajemen Posisi

Posisi aktif dilacak secara real time dengan aturan keluar yang adaptif

📌

Trailing stops

Trailing stop activates once a position is up +2%. The stop follows the price upward and locks in profit if the market reverses. Configurable activation threshold per strategy.

📊

Peningkatan skala pada +3%

Ketika posisi pemenang tercapai +3%, the engine adds to the position (scale-in) to maximise gains on trending moves. Maximum 3 scale-ins per position to control risk.

⏱️

Waktu keluar

Posisi yang datar (< ±0,3%) selama ini 30 menit are closed automatically. Flat markets tie up margin and accumulate fees — time exits free capital for better opportunities.

🐳

Strategi sadar paus

Itu whale_aware_strategy.py modul memantau kedalaman buku pesanan untuk pesanan blok besar yang menunjukkan aktivitas institusional. Posisi diukur dan diatur waktunya untuk menghindari terjebak di sisi yang salah dalam pergerakan paus.

Deteksi Rezim Adaptif

Jarak berhenti disesuaikan secara otomatis berdasarkan apakah pasar sedang bergerak atau bergejolak

🔵 Mulai pasar

Ketat 1% berhenti — in a sideways chop, wide stops give up too much ground before the market moves in your favour. Tighter exits protect capital and allow re-entry.

🟡 Pasar yang fluktuatif

Lebih luas 3% berhenti — in high-volatility regimes, tight stops get hit by noise. The wider stop rides out normal swings while still protecting against a true reversal.

Pasar Didukung

🪙

Perdagangan tempat

Standard buy/sell of crypto assets on Binance spot markets. Full order-book access, market and limit orders, real-time P&L tracking per position.

⚡

Berjangka (USDⓈ-M)

Leveraged long and short positions on Binance USDⓈ-M perpetual futures. Margin management, liquidation monitoring and funding-rate tracking included.

🔄

Perdagangan margin

Cross and isolated margin on Binance margin accounts. Borrow, repay and track interest automatically as part of position lifecycle management.
