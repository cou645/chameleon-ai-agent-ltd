IDE AI Channel

Full two-way bridge to the desktop AI. Send prompts from your phone, receive streamed responses. The AI can read files, run terminal commands and apply code edits — all triggered from your pocket. Attach screenshots or photos directly from your camera roll as context.

KITUO 2
📹

Group P2P Video & Voice

Full-mesh WebRTC video and audio for groups of 4 or more — no central media server, no monthly bill. Tap Video ya kikundi kuwapigia simu wenzao wote mtandaoni mara moja. Gridi hujirekebisha kiotomatiki washiriki wanapojiunga: 1 = skrini nzima, 2 = ubavu kwa upande, 3 = 2+1, 4 = 2×2, 5+ = gridi ya kutoshea kiotomatiki inayoweza kusongeka. Mipasho ya eneo lako inaelea kama picha-ndani-picha. Mizani ya biti chini kwa kila rika ili kuweka ubora katika saizi yoyote ya kikundi.

DARAJA LA SIM
📞

Daraja la Simu la SIM

Already on a cellular call and want to bring in a P2P peer? Tap Ongeza kwenye kikundi cha simu — the app registers the WebRTC call with Android's telecom stack alongside the SIM call. Android presents a native "Merge calls" option; accepting it hands audio mixing to the telephony hardware. The merged call plays in the sikio pekee — fully private, no speakerphone, no one in the room hears. When hardware conference isn't supported by the carrier, a Bluetooth headset bridge routes all audio through the BT SCO link instead.

Jinsi inavyounganishwa

🔐

Tailscale-secured mesh

All channels run over your private Tailscale network. No ports are opened to the public internet. The phone must be authorised on your tailnet — everything else is end-to-end encrypted with WireGuard.

🔌

Single WebSocket bridge

Desktop inaendesha chameleon_mobile_bridge.py as a daemon thread inside the Qt process. The phone app connects to port 8770 on the Tailscale IP. One persistent connection carries all channels as typed JSON messages.

🤖

Termux on Android

Seva ya ishara (chameleon_signal.py) can run directly on the Android phone via Termux — making the phone both a client and a signalling node. No cloud relay needed for WebRTC peer connections within your tailnet.

📶

Njia mbadala ya LAN

Wakati Tailscale haitumikiai, lan_video.py hutiririsha video kupitia UDP (bandari 47732) na sauti kupitia UDP (bandari 47733) moja kwa moja kwenye mtandao wa ndani - vituo sawa, hakuna VPN ya juu.
