Masomo ya Video ya Moja kwa Moja

📹

WebRTC group video — 4+ participants

Low-latency full-mesh WebRTC video and audio for groups of 4 or more — no central media server, no monthly cost. The Chameleon signal server (chameleon_signal.py) handles SDP and ICE relay (port 8766) and runs on Termux on Android too. The Companion app shows an adaptive grid that grows automatically as students join: 1 = full screen, 2 = side-by-side, 3 = 2+1, 4 = 2×2, 5+ = scrollable auto-fit grid — with the teacher's local feed always in a picture-in-picture.

🔐

Tailscale-secured connections

All peers connect through your Tailscale mesh — no public IP, no port forwarding. The IDE manages ACLs, device authorisation and auth key generation without leaving the app.

🎭

Integrated Kameleon Presenter

Teach with the full Kameleon stage during your lesson. Students can push answers, drawings and images directly to a Stage-mode stack — moderate and display in real time without switching apps.

🗣️

Wijeti ya matamshi

Wijeti ya kufundisha matamshi iliyojengwa ndani (pronunciation_widget.py) yenye maoni ya kiwango cha fonimu — bora kwa ufundishaji wa lugha pale ambapo utamkaji sahihi ni muhimu.

🌐

Njia mbadala ya video ya LAN

When Tailscale is unavailable, lan_video.py streams video over UDP (port 47732), audio over UDP (port 47733) and collaborative diffs over WebSocket (port 47731) — all on the local network.

Telecom & SIM Call Bridge

Unganisha simu za programu na simu halisi za SIM kwenye mazungumzo moja ya njia nyingi - moja kwa moja kutoka kwa programu ya Mwenzi

📞

Aina mbili za simu - mazungumzo moja

Programu ya Companion inasaidia njia mbili za simu zinazofanana: an Simu ya Programu (WebRTC, peer-to-peer over Tailscale, free) and a Simu ya SIM (standard mobile network, any phone number). You can be on a WebRTC call with a peer na wakati huo huo piga simu ya SIM kwa wahusika wengine, kisha wakutane pamoja kwa mazungumzo ya njia nyingi - bila kuacha programu.

📊

Upau wa hali ya mawasiliano ya simu

Upau wa hali unaoendelea katika sehemu ya juu ya programu ya Companion huonyesha hali ya moja kwa moja ya vituo vyote viwili: bila kufanya kitu, kupiga simu, kupiga simu au kunyamazishwa. Kuangalia mara moja tu kutakuambia ni simu zipi zinazotumika. Kila kituo kina vidhibiti huru vya kunyamazisha na kuzima — kuzima simu ya SIM hakukati simu ya programu, na kinyume chake.

🔀

Mtiririko wa somo la njia nyingi

Useful for language lessons: put a student on a WebRTC app call, then dial a native speaker on SIM for a spontaneous real-world conversation. All three parties can hear each other. The teacher moderates via the app while Kameleon Presenter continues running on the IDE.

🔴

Sheria ya kurekodi simu za Uingereza - wahusika wote lazima waarifiwe

Chini ya RIPA 2000 na Kanuni za Mawasiliano (Mazoezi halali ya Biashara), mhusika anayerekodi simu anaweza kufanya hivyo bila idhini ya mhusika mwingine kwa matumizi yake halali - lakini ili kurekodi kurekodiwe. inaruhusiwa mahakamani pande zote lazima zifahamishwe kabla ya kurekodi kuanza. Programu ya Companion inaonyesha maarufu 🔴 KUREKODI bendera kwa washiriki wote waliounganishwa wakati wowote kurekodi kunatumika. Kwa wanaopiga SIM, arifa ya mdomo inahitajika mwanzoni mwa simu. Data ya sauti ni data ya kibinafsi chini ya GDPR na lazima ishughulikiwe ipasavyo.

Malipo na Uandikishaji

Kubali malipo kutoka kwa wanafunzi duniani kote kabla ya kutoa ufikiaji wa somo

💳 Stripe (GBP msingi)
🟦 Alipay (Wanafunzi wa China)
🅿️ PayPal (rudi nyuma)
📚

Kozi na uandikishaji DB

Kozi na uandikishaji wa wanafunzi uliohifadhiwa ndani ~/.chameleon/courses.db. The AI can create courses, list enrolments, check payment status and revoke access — all via IDE commands.

💰

Msaada wa sarafu nyingi

Stripe handles GBP payments from UK and international students. Alipay targets Chinese students paying in CNY. PayPal provides a universal fallback with broad country coverage.

📸

Malipo ya picha ya skrini ya WeChat

For students without card access, accept WeChat Pay proof-of-payment screenshots. The AI reads the image, extracts the amount and reference, and logs the manual payment against the enrolment.

🔗

Viungo vya malipo

Generate Stripe-hosted payment links for any course. Share the link — student pays — enrolment is created automatically. No checkout page to build or host.
