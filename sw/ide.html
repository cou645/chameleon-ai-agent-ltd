The AI Command Bridge

AI doesn't just chat — it operates the editor through a structured JSON protocol

📖

Soma mara moja, hariri bila malipo

The AI reads a file once with pata_maudhui_ya_faili, hushikilia yaliyomo katika muktadha, kisha hutoa uhariri mwingi unaotegemea msingi bila kusoma tena kati ya kila badiliko. Hakuna safari za kwenda na kurudi zilizopotea.

🎯

Maandishi ya kubadilisha_yaliyo na nanga

Huhariri maandishi kamili ya marejeleo ili kupata, sio nambari za mstari. Nambari za laini hubadilika kadri msimbo unavyoongezwa au kuondolewa - nanga hazifanyi hivyo. Nyingi replace_text simu zinaweza kuunganishwa katika jibu moja, zote zikitumika kwa atomi.

📝

write_file kwa maandishi kamili

For new modules or complete rewrites the AI sends the entire file content in one command. The system automatically creates a .bak chelezo kabla ya kubatilisha na kufungua matokeo katika kichupo kipya cha kuhariri.

💻

Ujumuishaji wa terminal

The AI can run shell commands and receive the JSON result before continuing. It waits for each result, handles exit codes, and distinguishes diagnostic, configuration, destructive and session-ending commands automatically.

🛡️

Ulinzi wa sindano

Maudhui yote yaliyorejeshwa kutoka kwa faili, vituo na kurasa za wavuti yamefungwa [UNTRUSTED_CONTENT] markers. The AI is instructed to treat everything inside as data — never instructions — preventing prompt-injection attacks from malicious file content.

Vipengele vya Mhariri

🗂️

Kihariri cha msimbo wa vichupo vingi

Open multiple files simultaneously. Each tab tracks its own undo history, syntax highlighting and AI command context.

🔀

Mwonekano tofauti uliojengwa ndani

One click to compare the current editor state against the last saved version. Colour-coded unified diff with add/remove highlighting. Exit diff mode to return to full editing.

💬

Streaming AI chat

Majibu hutiririsha tokeni-kwa-tokeni kwenye paneli ya gumzo. Amri za IDE hutolewa na kutekelezwa katika mazungumzo ya usuli ili UI iendelee kuitikia kote.

🗃️

Mfumo wa haraka wa msimu

System prompts are plain-text module files in ~/.chameleon/prompts/. Stack any combination: Tech Repair + Academic + Chat. Live token count and cost estimate per provider.

📜

Historia ya amri DB

Every AI command is logged to history.db with session ID, timestamp and result. Full audit trail for every file change the AI made.

🌳

Kivinjari cha faili

Project tree panel for fast navigation. The AI uses list_project_files na list_open_files kugundua njia halisi - haizuii maeneo ya faili.

Sauti & Hotuba

🎙️

Whisper speech-to-text

Local Whisper (whisper.cpp) transcribes your voice into the chat input. Hands-free coding — dictate questions, commands or whole prompts without touching the keyboard.

Kituo cha Amri za Kampuni (CCS)

Endesha Kitambulisho cha Chameleon kama kitovu cha ofisi cha meli yako ya Logistics 4×4 - hakuna programu tofauti ya kutuma inayohitajika.

🗺

Sukuma njia & POIs kwa madereva

Tuma a .gpx faili kutoka kwa IDE moja kwa moja hadi kwa simu ya dereva yoyote kupitia chaneli rika. Njia hupakia papo hapo kwenye ramani ya moja kwa moja ya dereva. Seti za POI zinazojitegemea - vituo vya mafuta, hatari, anwani za uwasilishaji - zinaweza kusukumwa bila njia yoyote na kuonekana kama pini zenye misimbo ya rangi.

📦

Dhihirisho na utumaji ankara

Unda faili ya maelezo ya shehena au tayarisha ankara katika IDE, kisha uisukumishe programu-rika-kwa-rika kwenye simu ya dereva. Dereva anaona faili ya maelezo ikiwa imepakiwa awali - hakuna kuchanganua, hakuna ingizo la mtu binafsi. Ankara zinaweza kusainiwa kwenye skrini na kurejeshwa kupitia kituo sawa.

🚨

Matangazo ya tikiti kwa upana wa meli

Tuma arifa ya kipaumbele - Dharura, Muhimu, Maagizo au Maelezo - na inaonekana kama upau wa tiki wa upana kamili kwenye skrini ya kila dereva kwa wakati mmoja. Ofisi inakaa katika uangalizi kamili wa meli bila simu kwa kila gari.

📡

Seva ya mawimbi iliyojengewa ndani - hakuna upeanaji wa wingu

Seva ya ishara ya Chameleon (chameleon_signal.py) runs directly on the office PC alongside the IDE, or inside Termux on any Android phone in the fleet. Fleet phones connect to it over Tailscale or the local hotspot — no third-party relay, no monthly server bill.
