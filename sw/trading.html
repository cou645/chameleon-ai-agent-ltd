Usimamizi wa Nafasi

Nafasi amilifu zinazofuatiliwa kwa wakati halisi kwa kutumia sheria za kutoka

📌

Trailing stops

Trailing stop activates once a position is up +2%. The stop follows the price upward and locks in profit if the market reverses. Configurable activation threshold per strategy.

📊

Kuongeza kwa +3%

Wakati nafasi ya kushinda inafikia +3%, the engine adds to the position (scale-in) to maximise gains on trending moves. Maximum 3 scale-ins per position to control risk.

⏱️

Muda unatoka

Vyeo ambavyo vimekuwa tambarare (< ± 0.3%) kwa Dakika 30 are closed automatically. Flat markets tie up margin and accumulate fees — time exits free capital for better opportunities.

🐳

Mkakati wa kufahamu nyangumi

The whale_aware_strategy.py moduli hufuatilia kina cha kuagiza kitabu kwa maagizo makubwa ya vitalu ambayo yanaonyesha shughuli za kitaasisi. Vyeo hupimwa na kuwekewa muda ili kuepuka kukamatwa kwa upande usiofaa wa miondoko ya nyangumi.

Utambuzi wa Udhibiti Unaobadilika

Kuacha umbali kurekebisha kiotomatiki kulingana na kama soko ni tofauti au tete

🔵 Soko linaloendelea

Kaza 1% kuacha — in a sideways chop, wide stops give up too much ground before the market moves in your favour. Tighter exits protect capital and allow re-entry.

🟡 Soko tete

Kwa upana zaidi 3% kuacha — in high-volatility regimes, tight stops get hit by noise. The wider stop rides out normal swings while still protecting against a true reversal.
