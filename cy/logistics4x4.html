Sgrîn cwmpawd — llywio gan gymheiriaid all-lein

No data? The full-screen compass rose shows a bearing arrow for each online peer using last-known GPS. Peer distances and compass headings update from cached locations — useful for trekkers, off-road convoy navigation and mountain rescue coordination.

Haen Pwyntiau o Ddiddordeb (POI).

Tair ffynhonnell POIs wedi'u dangos fel pinnau â chôd lliw ar y map. Ambr pins yn dod o <wpt> cyfeirbwyntiau wedi'u mewnosod mewn ffeil GPX - maen nhw'n llwytho gyda'r llwybr ac yn clirio ag ef. Coch pins are dropped by the driver with a long-press on the map. All POIs persist across sessions; tap any pin to navigate, open in Maps, or delete. Icons adapt to type: ⛽ fuel, 🚚 delivery, ⚠️ hazard, 🏥 hospital, 🅿️ parking, ✈️ airport and more.

CYFEILLION
📹

Group P2P Video

Full-mesh WebRTC video and audio for up to 4+ peers — no central media server, no monthly call bill. Adaptive grid: 1 peer = full screen, 2 = side-by-side, 3 = 2+1, 4 = 2×2. Local feed floats as picture-in-picture. Bitrate scales per peer (1.5 Mbps → 350 Kbps) to keep quality even at every group size. Incoming calls ring and vibrate even when the app is in the background.

Pont Galw SIM

In a cellular call and want to pull in a P2P peer? Tap Ychwanegu at y grŵp — Android's telecom stack registers the WebRTC session alongside the SIM call and presents a native "Merge calls" option. Audio mixing happens in the telephony chip — clustffon yn unig, fully private. When the device or carrier doesn't support hardware conference, Bluetooth SCO headset routing is used as a fallback.

COMMS
🗣️

Gwthio-i-Siarad + Cyfieithu Byw

Hold the PTT button to speak — Android's speech recogniser (16 kHz, 16-bit) transcribes to text in real time. The detected language is sent to an on-device Argos Cyfieithu server. Each listener receives the message translated into their own selected locale and read aloud by TTS. Partial transcriptions stream as a live typing indicator while recording. Works over the P2P channel or standalone over a hotspot.

Modd Argyfwng - SMS a Sianel Cyfoedion

Protocol cod cryno sy'n gweithio dros SMS pan fydd data wedi diflannu, neu dros sianel gyfoedion y fflyd pan nad yw

📟

Cludiant deuol - SMS neu sianel cyfoedion

Pan nad oes data symudol - yn ddwfn yn y llwyn, ar groesfan ffin anghysbell, neu yn ystod toriad rhwydwaith - mae Logisteg 4 × 4 yn anfon fframiau statws wedi'u codio dros SMS safonol. Pan fydd data yn available, the same frames travel over the fleet peer channel at full speed. A per-send selector lets you choose SMS yn unig, Cyfoedion yn unig, neu Y ddau. SOS always fires on every available channel simultaneously regardless of the setting.

👥

Tri phroffil - un iaith a rennir

Mae codau wedi'u trefnu'n dri phroffil defnyddiwr sy'n rhannu geirfa rybuddio gyffredin: D— Gyrrwr (ffiniol, cargo, confoi, tanwydd, amodau ffyrdd), H—Hiciwr (vitals, trail, gear, check-in schedule), and X — Gwasanaethau Brys (brysbennu, math o ddigwyddiad, adnoddau yn y lleoliad, statws gwacáu). Mae pawb yn deall SOS o unrhyw broffil.

Wedi'i alinio â safonau brys rhyngwladol
