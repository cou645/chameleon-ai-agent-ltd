Trailing stops

Trailing stop activates once a position is up +2%. The stop follows the price upward and locks in profit if the market reverses. Configurable activation threshold per strategy.

📊

Graddfa i mewn ar +3%

Pan fydd safle buddugol yn cyrraedd +3%, the engine adds to the position (scale-in) to maximise gains on trending moves. Maximum 3 scale-ins per position to control risk.

⏱️

Amser allanfeydd

Swyddi sydd wedi bod yn wastad (< ±0.3%) 30 munud are closed automatically. Flat markets tie up margin and accumulate fees — time exits free capital for better opportunities.

Canfod Cyfundrefn Addasol

Mae pellteroedd stopio yn addasu'n awtomatig yn seiliedig ar a yw'r farchnad yn amrywio neu'n gyfnewidiol

🔵 Marchnad amrywiol

Yn dynn 1% yn stopio — in a sideways chop, wide stops give up too much ground before the market moves in your favour. Tighter exits protect capital and allow re-entry.

🟡 Marchnad gyfnewidiol

Ehangach 3% yn stopio — in high-volatility regimes, tight stops get hit by noise. The wider stop rides out normal swings while still protecting against a true reversal.
