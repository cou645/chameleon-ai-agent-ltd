Planul de conturi din Regatul Unit

Standard UK account codes out of the box — assets, liabilities, equity, income and expenses mapped to HMRC categories. Extend with custom accounts as needed.

🗄️

SQLite ledger

Toate datele stocate în ~/.chameleon/accounting/<company>/ledger.db. Funcționează complet offline. Asistență pentru mai multe companii — comutați între entitățile cu un semnalizator de comandă.

📅

Anul fiscal din Marea Britanie

Year-end correctly set to 5 April (UK fiscal year 6 Apr – 5 Apr). P&L and VAT reports automatically span the correct period without manual date adjustment.

🔍

Scanarea anomaliilor criminalistice

Fugi --forensic to detect unbalanced entries, duplicate references, VAT mismatches and other irregularities. Results printed as structured JSON for AI review.

🤖

AI-readable output

All reports output structured JSON to stdout so the Chameleon AI can parse, summarise and advise on your financial position in natural language.

🌐

WebSocket bridge

The accounting WebSocket bridge (acct_ws_bridge.py) exposes ledger queries to the mobile companion and external dashboards over your Tailscale network.

Rapoarte

📈

Profit și Pierdere

Venituri vs cheltuieli pentru perioada respectivă. Vânzări, costul mărfurilor, profit brut, cheltuieli de exploatare, profit net - toate defalcate după codul de cont. Alerga cu --report pl.

⚖️

Bilanţ

Active, pasive și capitaluri proprii la un moment dat. Confirmă integritatea cu intrare dublă - activele trebuie să fie egale cu pasive plus capitaluri proprii. Alerga cu --report bs.

🧾

VAT Return Summary

Box-by-box VAT figures matching the HMRC MTD VAT return format: Box 1 (output tax), Box 4 (input tax), Box 6 (net sales), Box 7 (net purchases). Run with --report vat.

Facturare

HMRC Notice 700-compliant invoices — printed, saved as PDF or pushed to your phone in one click

🧾

Câmpuri de factură conforme cu Regatul Unit

Every invoice includes all fields required by HMRC Notice 700: unique sequential invoice number, supplier name and address, VAT registration number, invoice date, date of supply (time of supply), client name and address, client VAT number where applicable, itemised line descriptions with quantities and unit prices, VAT rate per line, net and VAT subtotals, and total amount due. Nothing is missing, nothing is guessed.

🏢

Configurarea companiei

Deschide Fișier → Facturi → Configurare companie to enter your registered company name, address, Companies House number, VAT number, email, phone and bank details. Settings are saved per client database in company.json alături de registru și se încarcă automat ori de câte ori sunt generate facturi pentru acel client.

👥

Baza de date de contacte clienți

The first time you invoice a client their name, address and VAT number are saved to a contacts table inside the ledger. On subsequent invoices, typing the client name autocompletes their details — no re-entry needed. Contacts are editable at any time.

🖨️

Imprimați, PDF și trimiteți la telefon

De la Managerul de facturi (Fișier → Facturi…) selectați orice factură și alegeți: Imprimare (deschide dialogul de imprimare a sistemului de operare — orice driver de imprimantă sau PDF), Salvați PDF (direct pe disc prin exportul PDF încorporat al Qt) sau Trimite la telefon (pushes the invoice HTML to the Companion app where it opens the Android print dialog — print wirelessly to any AirPrint or nearby printer).

📅

Data aprovizionării

Separate "Date of supply" (time of supply) field — distinct from invoice date. Required by HMRC for VAT purposes. Defaults to today but is independently editable per invoice.

📱

Imprimare factura mobila

Aplicația Companion poate imprima orice factură fără fir. Atingeți pictograma de imprimare pe orice rând de factură - aplicația preia codul HTML din IDE, îl convertește în PDF pe dispozitiv prin intermediul printing package, and opens the Android system print dialog.

✅

HMRC Notice 700 compliance

All 12 required fields for a full UK VAT invoice are present and populated. Reduced-rate and zero-rated lines are clearly labelled. The invoice number sequence is strictly monotone-increasing with no gaps.
