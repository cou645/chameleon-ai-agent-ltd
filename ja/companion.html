Full-mesh WebRTC video and audio for groups of 4 or more — no central media server, no monthly bill. SIMブリッジ
📞

SIM コール ブリッジ

Already on a cellular call and want to bring in a P2P peer? Tap 通話グループに追加 — the app registers the WebRTC call with Android's telecom stack alongside the SIM call. Android presents a native "Merge calls" option; accepting it hands audio mixing to the telephony hardware. The merged call plays in the イヤホンのみ — fully private, no speakerphone, no one in the room hears. When hardware conference isn't supported by the carrier, a Bluetooth headset bridge routes all audio through the BT SCO link instead.

接続方法

🔐

Tailscale-secured mesh

All channels run over your private Tailscale network. No ports are opened to the public internet. The phone must be authorised on your tailnet — everything else is end-to-end encrypted with WireGuard.

🔌

Single WebSocket bridge

デスクトップが動く chameleon_mobile_bridge.py as a daemon thread inside the Qt process. The phone app connects to port 8770 on the Tailscale IP. One persistent connection carries all channels as typed JSON messages.

🤖

Termux on Android

シグナルサーバー (chameleon_signal.py) can run directly on the Android phone via Termux — making the phone both a client and a signalling node. No cloud relay needed for WebRTC peer connections within your tailnet.

📶

LANフォールバック

Tailscale が利用できない場合、ai lan_video.py ビデオは UDP (ポート 47732) 経由で、オーディオは UDP (ポート 47733) 経由でローカル ネットワークに直接ストリーミングされます。同じチャネルであり、VPN オーバーヘッドはありません。
